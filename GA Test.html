Help Chipotle win some Webbys.

The Chipotle app redesign, launched back in 2021, has been nominated for two Webbys. Now we need your help to clinch the People's Voice award. Get in there and vote us up to the top in these two categories: Best Food and Drink and Best User Interface. Voting ends April 21st. Thanks for the support!
